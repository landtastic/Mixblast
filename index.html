Mixblast is an experimental, text-based, streaming music player.

It does two things:

1. It turns a list of songs into a playlist (copy + paste a text list of songs, blast a mix)

2. It makes mixes! Try out the top search bar to add songs of artists you like. You can add related artists after you search.


Mixes are saved automatically under recent history (small clock icon).

You can also save your mix to a Youtube playlist after the playlist is done loading.

Keyboard Shortcuts: Spacebar = Play/Pause, Left Arrow = Back, Right Arrow = Next, ~ = Open/Close Text List Editor

iPhone instructions (currently not working): open in Safari, tap "Add to Home Screen", launch Mixblast from Home screen.

Mixblast uses the Youtube, Last.fm, and Echo Nest APIs.
